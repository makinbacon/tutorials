Make an animation for Youtube

Last edited on 1st January 2016


You've seen cartoons and animations on the TV, right? Well, wouldn't it be cool to learn how to make your own? This tutorial will teach you how to make an animation from your computer!


Downloading and setting up


Step 1: Go to plotagon.com and click download to download their software.

Step 2: Once you have finished downloading, open the installer file and just run it.

Step 3: When the program has finished installing, open it!

Step 4: When you open it, press the button that says "Sign Up by Email."

Step 5: Fill in the little form and hit "Sign Up!"


Making your animation


Step 6: This bit's easy. Press "Create a Plot"

Step 7: Press "Scene" and look through the list of scenes for your movie and pick one!

Step 8: Now pick your characters

Step 9: Press "Dialogue", pick a character, and type what you want them to say!

Step 10: Repeat Step 9 until story is finished! You can add sound and music too!


Share it!

Step 11: Press "Publish and Share"

Step 12: Decide a name and publish it!

Step 13: Share it on things like Facebook if you want to!

Step 14: It will also be published to your plotagon account page! (plotagon.com/ YOUR USERNAME HERE)
