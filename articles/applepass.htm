Set password for your Apple device

Last edited: 1st January 2016


Want to stay secure on apple devices? Set a password! You can set a word password or a 4-digit number password!


Word password


Step 1: Go to Settings

Step 2: From there, press Passcode

Step 3: If you already have a password, you have to enter it in.

Step 4: Turn off simple passcode

Step 5: Enter the word


4-digit number password


Step 1: Go to Settings

Step 2: From there, press Passcode

Step 3: If you already have a password, you have to enter it in.

Step 4: Turn on simple passcode

Step 5: Enter the 4-digit number of your choice!
