Take photos and videos with your phone's camera!

Last edited: 2nd January 2016


See something nice outside? Take a picture of it with your phone!


Step 1: Turn on your phone.

Step 2: Look for an application called camera and click on it.

Step 3: Aim the phone at the item you want to take a picture of.

Step 4: Press the video or photo button.

Step 5: Done!
