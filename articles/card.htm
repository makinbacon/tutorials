Design a greeting card

Last edited: 1st January 2016


Maybe it's your friends birthday, or maybe something else! Why not make them a card?


Step 1: Get a piece of paper or card and fold it in half

Step 2: On the front part write a message and a picture

Step 3: Make it colourful

Step 4: Inside the card write who it's to and who it's from!

Step 5: Post it or give it to your friend!
