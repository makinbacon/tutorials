Set up email!

Last edited: 1st January 2016


Email is a great way to stay in contact with your friends! This tutorial will show you how to sign up!


Step 1: Go to hotmail.com

Step 2: Press Sign up

Step 3: Fill in the sign up form

Step 4: Give your email address to friends and ask friends for their email addresses

Step 5: Now you have email! Write down the email and password somewhere and keep the password a secret!
