Code a fake message with VBScript!

Last edited: 2nd January 2016


Want to trick your friends that their computers are broken? Try this!


Step 1: Open Notepad

Step 2: Paste this in: x=msgbox("Your Message Here", 0+16, "Your Title Here")

Step 3: Replace Your Title Here with an error title (example: Oh no!)

Step 4: Replace Your Message Here with the error message (example: The computer is broken!)

Step 5: Press "Save as" and set the title as Error.vbs but don't press save yet!

Step 6: Now where it says "Text Document (txt) click on it and press All Files. Now Press Save!

Step 7: Run the file for your fake error!
