Want to make some music? Here's how:


Step 1: Go to https://soundation.com/studio

Step 2: Now look to the right of the site when it loads up.

Step 3: The folder called Free Sounds is open

Step 4: From there, press the Electronica folder to open it up and then the 125 folder. Now you have lots of sounds. Drag them into the little timeline.

Step 5: To save your song press the "File" menu, then "Export .wav file"
