How to make Toast

Last edited: 1st January 2016


Toast is a great snack! Here's how to make the perfect slice!


Step 1: Toast your bread. Leave it so it is the slightest bit overdone.

Step 2: Once out of the toaster, put the butter on the toasted bread straight away. It will melt on the slightly overdone bread because it's hot.

Step 3: Cut it (into 4 square quarters or 4 triangular quarters.)

Step 4: Serve on a plate.

Step 5: Enjoy!
